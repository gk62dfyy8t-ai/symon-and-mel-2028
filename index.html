Chapter One

A Chance Meeting

Every story has a beginning.

Ours started in a local bar, thanks to a mutual friend who clearly knew something we didn't.

She wandered over and asked me a simple question.

"Are you single?"

When I answered yes, she smiled.

"Great... I'll be back in a minute."

Moments later she returned with Mel.

We only had a few minutes to talk before I had to disappear with friends, and before I knew it, the evening was over.

There was just one problem... I'd forgotten to ask for her number.

The following day I messaged our mutual friend, hoping she could rescue my missed opportunity.

Thankfully, she did.

Instead of dinner or coffee, our first dates happened somewhere a little less traditional.

🎮 Xbox Live

We spent evenings playing games, chatting for hours and getting to know one another.

Looking back, it was probably our first date...
just with controllers instead of candles.
