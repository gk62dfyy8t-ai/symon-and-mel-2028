Our Story

Every great adventure has a beginning.
This is ours.

A Chance Meeting

Every story has a beginning.

Ours started in a local bar thanks to a mutual friend who clearly knew something we didn't.

She wandered over and asked me one simple question.

"Are you still single?"

When I answered yes, she smiled.

"Great... I'll be back in a minute."

A few moments later she returned with Mel.

We only had a few minutes to talk before I disappeared with my friends and, before I knew it, the evening was over.

There was just one problem...

I'd forgotten to ask for her number.

The following day I messaged our mutual friend hoping she could rescue my missed opportunity.

Thankfully... she did.

Ready Player 2

Most couples have dinner dates.

Ours happened on Xbox Live.

Night after night we jumped into party chat, played games and spent hours talking.

Looking back...

They were probably our first dates...
just with controllers instead of candles.

Before long we finally met properly in person and one of our favourite dates took us to NQ64.

I'd like to tell everyone I put up a respectable fight.

Mel remembers it slightly differently.

She absolutely destroyed me.

Then she casually mentioned her Xbox Gamerscore.

350,000 Gamerscore.

That was the moment I realised I wasn't just dating someone who enjoyed gaming...

I was dating someone who genuinely shared one of my biggest passions.

These days she's passed one million Gamerscore... and she's still better than me.

As if that wasn't enough, she also baked her way into my heart with cakes, madeleines and enough sweet treats to make any diet completely impossible.

Building Our World

As our relationship grew, so did our little world.

Meeting Mel's children was something completely new for me, but it didn't take long before they became one of the best parts of my life.

Family movie nights.

Days out together.

Gaming together.

Laughing together.

The little everyday moments slowly became our favourite memories.

Without really noticing...

"Mine" quietly became "Ours".

Then came one of those moments I'll never forget.

One day, completely out of the blue, Llana quietly called me Dad.

I remember leaning over to Mel with the biggest smile on my face and whispering...

"Did she just call me Dad?"

She had.

I already had one daughter.

Now somehow I had two...

...and a son to match.

That tiny moment meant more to me than she'll probably ever know.

Every Four Years

Mel has one of the rarest birthdays there is.

She was born on the 29th of February.

Every Leap Day there's an old tradition that women propose to the men.

We don't really follow tradition...

So I threw that idea straight out of the window.

A few days before her birthday I gave her one clue.

"Your present will last a lifetime... but you have to accept it first."

She had absolutely no idea what I meant.

On her birthday I gave her a few small presents before handing over one final box.

I didn't get down on one knee.

I simply asked the biggest question of my life.

Mel cried.

Llana cried.

Eli jumped around the room celebrating.

She said yes.
